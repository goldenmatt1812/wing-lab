ⓘ How to take a great wing photo

1. Lay the wing flat on a plain, contrasting surface (white paper on a dark table, or dark paper for light wings).

2. Place a reference object next to the wing so AI can measure real-world size:

📏 Ruler (best!) — place along the wing, markings visible
💳 Credit card — 8.56 × 5.40 cm
📄 US letter paper — lay wing ON the paper (27.9 × 21.6 cm)
🪙 US quarter — 2.43 cm diameter (great for small wings)
✏️ Pencil — standard pencil = 19 cm long
📱 Phone — place your phone next to it (~7.1 cm wide)

3. Lighting: Even, diffused light. No harsh shadows across the wing. Near a window works great.

4. Angle: Shoot straight down from above (bird's-eye view). Keep your phone parallel to the table.

5. Frame it: Get the whole wing and the whole reference object in the shot. A little margin around the edges is fine.
